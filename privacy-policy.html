Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

CDL Test Prep Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Only:

  • Your quiz progress and scores (stored locally on your device)
  • Your selected state for state-specific questions (stored locally)
  • Your app preferences (theme, settings) (stored locally)

We DO NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

How We Use Your Information

All data collected by this app is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage. This data is used solely to:

  • Save your progress through practice questions
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide personalized study recommendations
  • Enable offline functionality

No data leaves your device. We do not have access to any information stored locally on your device.

Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored using browser local storage technology. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Can be cleared by clearing your browser data
  • Is automatically deleted if you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Google Play In-App Purchases

Our app offers optional premium subscriptions and one-time purchases through Google Play Billing. When you make a purchase:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play
  • We do not collect or store any payment information
  • Purchase status is verified through Google Play services
  • For subscription management, visit your Google Play subscriptions settings

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for individuals studying for their Commercial Driver's License, typically ages 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: You can view all stored data through the app interface
  • Delete: You can clear all app data by clearing browser storage or uninstalling
  • Control: You decide what information to save and when to clear it

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
